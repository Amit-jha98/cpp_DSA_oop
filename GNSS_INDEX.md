## 💡 Pro Tips

### For Best Results

1. **Don't Rush**: Quality publications take time
2. **Get Feedback**: Show to advisor and peers early
3. **Read References**: Don't just cite, actually read them
4. **Follow Format**: Use journal's LaTeX/Word template
5. **Be Honest**: Don't overclaim results
6. **Test Everything**: Verify all code and results
7. **Proofread**: Multiple times, different days
8. **Track Changes**: Keep versions of your paper

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ Submitting without advisor review  
❌ Ignoring journal formatting requirements  
❌ Citing papers you haven't read  
❌ Missing submission deadlines  
❌ Not including error bars/confidence intervals  
❌ Poor figure quality (below 300 DPI)  
❌ Inconsistent terminology  
❌ Forgetting to check for plagiarism
